About Us

About Us

Established in the year 2021, we "Sapa Surgical Care Pvt. Ltd." are dealing in surgical products. We are the sister concern business of Ajay Surgical. We have gradually marked place amongst the top companies dealing in Medical Products. The high demand of these Medical Products has led us to the inception and we have been successfully catering to the requirements of the clients residing across the globe. The comprehensive range of Medical Products includes, Disposable Surgical Products, Face Mask, Surgeon Cap, Bouffant Cap, Surgeon Hood Cap (Monkey Cap) Non-Woven, Latex Examination Powdered Gloves, Latex Examination Powdered free Gloves, Latex Surgical Gloves, Nitrile Gloves, Non-Woven Shoe Cover etc. Our warehouse is categorized into various sections in order to store the products as per their category. Our warehousing team is well-experienced and trained in standard warehousing procedures to handle all these chemicals. We also take into consideration the valuable feedback of our clients in order to improve our product quality.
